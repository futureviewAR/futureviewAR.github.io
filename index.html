About

FutureViewAR is a HoloLens application created during the VR EcoHack at Brookline Interactive Group that challenges users to make responsible choices to prevent pollution, water source acidification, and the impact of global climate change.


In September 2015 the United Nations adopted 17 Sustainable Development Goals. Our project focuses on various sustainability solutions, particularly: clean Water and sanitation, affordable and clean Energy, climate action, life on land and life below Water.
